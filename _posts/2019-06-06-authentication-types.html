Different ways to answer "Who am I?"

  1. Traditional (Username + Password)
  2. Enterprise (AD, LDAP, Google Apps, etc.)
  3. Social Login (Google, Facebook, Twitter, etc.)
  4. Client Certificates
  5. Multi-Factors Authentication
  6. Passwordless
